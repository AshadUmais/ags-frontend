AGS WonderWorld Ticket Booking – Refund Policy Last Updated: 16/11/25

1.  General Policy

-   All ticket sales made through www.agswonderworld.com are final and
    non-refundable, except in the limited cases mentioned below.
-   Rescheduling of tickets is not allowed under any circumstances.
-   Tickets are valid only for the selected date and cannot be
    transferred to another date or person.

2.  Refund Eligibility

2.1 Park Closure by Management A full refund will be provided if: - The
theme park is completely closed on the scheduled date due to
operational, safety, or regulatory reasons. If the park is operational,
no refund will be provided, even if certain rides or attractions are
unavailable.

2.2 Duplicate or Failed Transactions Refunds will be approved if: -
Payment was deducted but the ticket was not generated. - There were
duplicate payments or duplicate bookings.

3.  Non-Refundable Situations

3.1 Bad Weather - No refunds will be issued due to rain, heat, cold,
wind, or any weather-related conditions. - If the park remains open, the
weather does not qualify for a refund claim.

3.2 Personal Reasons - Change of plans or inability to attend. -
Illness, travel issues, transport delays, or emergencies. - Failure to
arrive on time or missed entry slots.

3.3 Ride/Attraction Unavailability - Temporary closure of specific
rides, shows, or attractions due to maintenance or weather does not
qualify for refunds.

3.4 Promotional Tickets - Tickets purchased during special offers,
discount campaigns, flash sales, or non-refundable promotions cannot be
refunded.

4.  Refund Request Process If your situation qualifies for a refund
    under this policy:

5.  Contact us at [support@agswonderworld.com] with:

    -   Booking ID
    -   Registered email/phone
    -   Reason for refund
    -   Proof (if required)

6.  The support team will verify and process eligible cases.

7.  Refund Processing Time

-   Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days.
-   Bank or payment gateway processing times may vary.
